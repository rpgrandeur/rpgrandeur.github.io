Titan is a large city located in the District of Titan of Delaware in the United States of America. Titan is famous for its forward-looking, optimistic spirit, captured in its nickname "City of Heroes".

It is home to the headquarters of both the League Of Heroes and the new UN as well as a number of the world's largest global corporations and Industries.

The Stock Exchange can also be found in Titan. It is the most advanced city in the 31st century and home to some of the greatest edifices and Pro Heroes of the modern era.

Titan is the capital of global Heroism and a first class seat to witness the vicissitudes of mutant crime and crime fighting.

History

Titan was founded in 1661 by early European settlers at the start of the colonization of North America. It grew steadily to become one of the largest cities in the world, and recently became a famous tech hub and media centre.

In the aftermath of the battle with the Voidwalkers, a large statue of Bliss Ada was built in the city center in a new purpose built venue called Heroes Park. The statue has since been joined by another, of Imran Ada (Saturn Girl) and together they are referred to as the Twin Patinas.

The city is currently under the watchful eye of the Pro Hero Zenith. This article will be updated if and when that changes.
